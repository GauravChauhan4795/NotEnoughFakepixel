# Privacy Policy for NEF

**Last Updated:** 3 June 2026

NotEnoughFakepixel does not collect, transmit, store, or sell personal data.

### 1. Data Collection
NEF no longer sends analytics, usernames, server information, mod lists, or other gameplay data to any external server.

### 2. Local Processing
Some features may inspect information locally on your client to function correctly. For example, the mod may check your installed mods to detect OptiFine and show a recommendation message. This processing stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere by NEF.

### 3. Third-Party Sharing
NEF does not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties because it does not collect that data in the first place.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
If NEF's privacy behavior changes in the future, this document should be updated before or at the same time as that change.

### 5. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns, open an issue on this GitHub repository or contact @im2pb.
